What types of cooking oils does Uplife offer?

India Gate Uplife offers two oil blends: Uplife Lite, comprising 80% refined rice bran oil and 20% refined sunflower oil, and Uplife Gutpro, consisting of 80% refined rice bran oil and 20% refined soybean oil.

02

What are the health benefits associated with Uplife cooking oils?

Uplife Lite offers lower fat intake that helps in less calorie consumption and Gamma-Oryzanol's antioxidants properties, while Uplife Gut pro aids digestion due to balanced fatty acids, gamma oryzanol, tocopherols, tocotrienols and phytosterols.

03

Are Uplife oils suitable for all types of cooking?

Yes, IG Uplife oils are versatile in nature, have neutral flavours and suitable for a wide range of cooking techniques, from frying and sautéing to baking and salad dressings.

04

Do Uplife oils contain any additives or preservatives?

Yes, we have added Anti-oxidants (INS 319, 330) that helps prevents oxidation, enhance stability and extend shelf life. An anti-foaming agent (INS 900a) is added to prevent and eliminate unwanted foam, maintaining optimal performance, safety, and quality.

05

Are Uplife oils cholesterol-free?

Yes, Uplife oils are cholesterol-free, which makes them suitable for a healthy diet. Uplife oils have a balanced fatty acid profile.

06

What is the shelf life of Uplife cooking oils?

Uplife cooking oils have a shelf life of 12 months. The oil may become hazy below 15°C due to its natural properties, but it remains safe for consumption. *Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.

Yes, Uplife oils are GMO-free.

Uplife oils are physically refined, which provides better retention of natural nutrients like gamma-oryzanol, tocopherols, and tocotrienols, while also maximizing yield without the use of chemicals.
